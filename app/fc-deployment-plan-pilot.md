## Page 3ï½œWhy this pilot exists

### The subscription problem FC is addressing

Content subscriptions rarely fail due to content quality.
They fail because **value fades between sessions**.

Common patterns we observe:

* Users intend to return, but forget
* Paid subscribers feel less perceived value before renewal
* Most touchpoints are ephemeral (inbox, feeds, notifications)

FCâ€™s core hypothesis is simple:

> **Persistent physical touchpoints can extend content recall and subscription intent over time.**

This pilot exists to test that hypothesis under real operating conditions.

## Page 5ï½œPlan choice

### Plan A â€” Growth Only

This pilot focuses primarily on **incremental subscriber growth** before attempting deeper retention optimization.

Why start with growth:

* Growth signals emerge faster and are easier to isolate
* Retention effects are typically delayed and require longer observation windows
* A growth-first pilot reduces interpretation ambiguity

Retention optimization can be layered in **after initial validation**.
